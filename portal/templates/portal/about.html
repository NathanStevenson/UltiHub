About Me
My name is Nathan Stevenson and I am the founder of UltiHub. I am currently a senior studying Computer Science at the University of Virginia. I am also a member of Virginia Night Train, which is the College Men's team at UVA. After two years of playing ultimate frisbee I have been a part of many club and college teams and noticed that there is a need for an organized and centralized platform where team leadership could easily update and relay information to their teams, which is why I created UltiHub. My goal is for this platform to be used across many teams, so if there is a feature not currently included that you would like to see please reach out to me via our Contact Page. Thank you for using this platform!
